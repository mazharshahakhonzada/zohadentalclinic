our dental services

Alignment specialist

Dealing with prevention, detection and treatment of deformities of the jaws and teeth. The treatment of malocclusion basically pursues the goal of avoiding and correcting long-term disorders in the chewing function with influences on the periodontium.

Cosmetic dentistry

More and more patients not only want healthy teeth, but also set high standards for dental aesthetics. The cosmetic dentistry offers many ways to achieve the goal of an aesthetic smile by beautiful teeth to achieve. We clarify the treatment spectrum of aesthetic dentistry.

Aesthetic dentistry

Aesthetic dentistry uses techniques available to modern medicine to give you a more natural, healthy look. Aesthetic dentists use techniques like regular cleanings, root canal removal, and the restoration of teeth with fillings or bridges to make your teeth look normal and healthy.

Root canal Treatment

A root canal treatment is basically the removal of all the necrotic pulp present inside the tooth (Pulpectomy) and filling the roots with one of the best sealants available around the world.

Dental Implants

Despite patient education and medical advancements, people still suffer from tooth loss. The perfect solution to that is dental implants, these screws integrate with your bone and can give you your function and smile back.

Dental surgery

We perform surgical procedures from wisdom tooth removal to root capping (WSR), cyst removal and soft tissue surgery. Also for the insertion of implants and the construction of bones we are well trained and equipped.
